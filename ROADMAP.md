# Pixel Earth — Roadmap

This document outlines the 10-phase development plan. Each phase ends with a review before the next begins.

---

## ✅ Phase 1 — Foundation
*Complete*

Engine loop, world generation, human AI, weather, save system, mod API, renderer, HUD.

---

## 🔜 Phase 2 — World Engine

- River generation (flow from mountains to ocean)
- Road network generation
- Village and city placement
- Resource node distribution (wood, stone, iron, gold, coal, oil)
- Bridges over rivers
- Farm plot generation near villages
- Beach and coastal refinements

---

## ⏳ Phase 3 — Entity Engine

- Animal species (wolves, deer, rabbits, bears, fish, birds, bees, horses, cows)
- Predator/prey behavior
- Bee pollination → crop growth cycle
- Plant growth, aging, and decay
- Body decomposition
- Full ecosystem interdependency

---

## ⏳ Phase 4 — Deep Human AI

- Marriage system
- Childbirth and family trees
- Education skill progression
- Criminal behavior and law enforcement
- Voting and civic participation
- Travel and migration
- Cultural identity and traditions
- Aging and retirement

---

## ⏳ Phase 5 — Economy

- Currency and pricing
- Businesses: shops, farms, factories
- Banking and loans
- Taxation system
- Supply and demand simulation
- Import/export between nations
- Stock market
- GDP tracking
- Poverty and wealth inequality
- Economic crashes and booms

---

## ⏳ Phase 6 — Politics

- Nation formation
- Government types: democracy, monarchy, dictatorship, communism
- Political parties
- Elections and voting
- Police and court system
- Military units
- Laws and crime penalties
- Diplomacy: alliances, sanctions, treaties
- Government budgets
- Corruption modeling

---

## ⏳ Phase 7 — Society

- School, college, university progression
- Research and technology unlocks
- Hospital system and disease
- Pandemics and vaccination campaigns
- Healthcare quality by nation
- Transport: walking → horses → carts → trains → cars → aircraft
- Religious institutions and beliefs
- Art and culture spread

---

## ⏳ Phase 8 — Revolution & War

- Protest events (triggered by inequality/stress)
- Riots and civil unrest
- Coups and government collapse
- Independence movements
- Civil wars
- Inter-nation wars with military units
- Battle simulation on terrain
- Peace treaties and post-war reconstruction
- Refugee movement

---

## ⏳ Phase 9 — Technology Progress

- Tech tree (Stone Age → Iron Age → Industrial → Digital)
- Research institutions unlock technologies
- Technologies change available jobs, buildings, transport
- Space age (late game)

---

## ⏳ Phase 10 — Polish & Optimization

- Web Worker offloading for simulation
- Object pooling for entities and graphics
- Mobile touch controls
- Minimap rendering
- History browser UI
- World statistics dashboard
- Multiplayer-ready architecture (client/server separation)
- Cloud save support
- Full mod documentation site
- Accessibility (reduced motion, keyboard nav)
- Performance: target 10,000+ simultaneous entities
